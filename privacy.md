
# Privacy Policy — Halal AI

*Last updated: May 18, 2026*

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account data**: email address and name via Google or Apple Sign-In.
- **Scan data**: photos and text you submit for halal analysis. These are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) and are not stored permanently.
- **Usage data**: number of free analyses used, subscription status.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide halal food analysis powered by AI.
- To manage your subscription and free trial.
- To authenticate you securely via Firebase.

## 3. Data Storage
Your account data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud). We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services
- **Firebase** (Google) — authentication and database
- **Anthropic** — AI analysis (images/text processed but not stored)
- **Apple App Store / Google Play** — payment processing

## 5. Data Retention
You can delete your account at any time by contacting us. All associated data will be deleted within 30 days.

## 6. Children
This app is not directed at children under 13.
